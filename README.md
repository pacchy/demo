# CareCircle

**Open the demo:** https://pacchy.github.io/demo/portal/

## What is CareCircle?

CareCircle is for families who want to stay organised with everyday healthcare — doctor visits, follow-up tests, and health paperwork.

## For families at home

- One place for everyone you care for — yourself, parents, children, and more
- Reminders for upcoming doctor visits
- Helpful nudges when a follow-up check might be due
- A simple history of visits, test results, and health notes

## What you can try in the demo

- Tap a name on the left to open that person’s health record
- See their upcoming visits and past appointments
- Look through their test results and visit history
- The names and results shown here are examples only

Choose a patient on the left to have a look around.
